• Preliminary Statement
    Brief Introduction

    Torsten Paulson (“Plaintiff”) is suing pursuant to the Family Medical Leave Act regarding his termination by Dracon Publishing, Inc. (“Dracon”). Since the undisputed facts as Plaintiff alleges them establish that he was fired for cause, and that his termination was lawful under that Act, Dracon is seeking summary judgment in the matter.
